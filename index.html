About Me

Hello, My name is Timothy Robinson. As a soon-to-be graduate of UNC Chapel-Hill, I am proud to be a Front End Developer. Over the past year, I have worked extensively on developing my front-end skills and have become proficient in HTML, CSS including libraries like Bootstrap, and Javascript. I have a variety of degrees including a Bachelors in Communication Studies and a Masters in Accounting. My passion for coding began in high school, where for my senior project I developed a web application to display information about various types of sharks. Although I thoroughly enjoyed that work, life pulled me in a slightly different direction and thus I became an Accountant. Quickly after beginning my career as an Accountant, I realized that something was missing. The PASSION. The spark and enthusiasm that I had when I did my first piece of coding was gone. After spending some time as an Accountant, I decided that it was time to get back the spark that I felt when I did my first piece of coding. Thus I went back to school at UNC Chapel Hill. Armed with all of my skills and knowledge, I am ecstatic to finally begin my career as a Front End Developer!
